About Mixie Technologies

A Platform-First Software Company

Mixie Technologies, Inc. is a technology-centered public company building a software ecosystem spanning intelligent media, digital twins, immersive visualization, spatial interfaces, and connected asset intelligence.

The operating model combines direct ownership of software businesses, strategic platform investments, and commercialization through deployable applications and recurring services — practical software for real operating environments.

Platform Portfolio

Four Platforms. One Ecosystem.

Wholly owned software businesses and strategic minority investments, united by a common commercialization model and deployment approach.

Wholly Owned Subsidiary

Mixie IP Holdings

The core software and IP layer behind all Mixie applications — intelligent media transport, AI/ML pipelines at the edge, computer-vision analytics, spatial & AR interfaces, MiXie LTMS, and enhanced video platforms including MiXie Media, Cloud, and Mobi.

Intelligent MediaAI / ML EdgeComputer VisionMiXie LTMSAR Interfaces
Wholly Owned Subsidiary

Mixie Labs

Productization and deployment — packaging core software engines into licensable SDKs and applications, then supporting adoption through onboarding, systems integration, and optional managed operations. The bridge between capability and field deployment.

SDK PackagingOnboardingSystems IntegrationManaged Ops
Minority Interest Investment

HoloTwin

Digital twin visualization consolidating operational data from networks, sensors, IoT, building systems, and physical infrastructure into a unified 3D and panel interface. Positioned for telecoms, real estate, healthcare, utilities, venues, and industrial operations.

Digital TwinsIoT Integration3D VisualizationOperations Intelligence

Mixie Realty

Immersive real-estate technology — configurable digital property models, interactive visualization, and connected-property operations covering security, HVAC, energy management, access control, and IoT-enabled workflows. Beyond static listings and basic 3D tours.

Where the Platforms Apply

Four operating environments where Mixie's software creates measurable operational value.

01

Infrastructure & Operations

A unified operational view across networks, sensors, power, security, and building controls — replacing disconnected tools for facility and infrastructure operators.

02

Real Estate & Property

Configurable digital property experiences for listing and evaluation, plus connected-property operations — security, HVAC, energy, and IoT workflows in one platform.

03

Healthcare & Training

MiXie LTMS and competency-based simulation for clinician and field-service training — performance telemetry and immersive learning with measurable outcomes.

04

Media, Venues & Communications

Intelligent media transport, immersive video, low-latency contribution, creator tools, fan engagement, and enterprise venue communications in one coordinated stack.

Investor FAQ

What does Mixie Technologies do?

Public company disclosures describe Mixie as a technology-centered company developing platform-based, AI-enabled software across intelligent media, immersive visualization, digital twin architecture, spatial interfaces, and connected asset intelligence.

Which businesses are wholly owned?

Current public disclosures identify Mixie IP Holdings, LLC and Mixie Labs, LLC as wholly owned subsidiaries of Mixie Technologies, Inc.

Which platforms are minority-interest investments?

HoloTwin (minority interest with rights to invest further) and Mixie Realty (25% minority interest with rights to invest further), per current public disclosures.

Where do official disclosures appear?

The Investor Relations section of this website, the company's OTC Markets profile and disclosure page, and press releases distributed through recognized newswire services.
